What is a Viral Hook?

A viral hook is the first sentence of your video that captures attention instantly. On platforms like TikTok, YouTube Shorts and Instagram Reels, you have less than 3 seconds to stop the scroll.

Examples of High-Converting Hooks

• Nobody talks about this in fitness.
• You’re doing trading completely wrong.
• If you’re serious about growing on TikTok, watch this.
• Stop scrolling if you care about making money online.
• This changed everything about my business.

How to Write Better Hooks for Short-Form Content

The best hooks create curiosity, tension or immediate value. Use pattern interrupts, challenge common beliefs, or promise a clear result.
